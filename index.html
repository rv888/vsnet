About

For over 20 years, we have offered innovative executive, software, and management solutions to startups and various industries including: corporate, real estate, entertainment, healthcare, manufacturing, food, and more. VersusMedia was founded by Ryan Vinson in 1999 after he worked for a number of dot-com companies across the US.

During the past five years, we have been deep in the trenches of the next big cutting edge technology, AI. We've created solutions for AI-based sentiment analysis fine tuned for specific keywords and phrases using large sets of customer data including scraping and scanning chat conversations, meeting transcripts, and more. Additionally, we've created methods using AI to understand audience research and production planning. Finally, we've used AI to help automate dozens of tasks and create imaginative dynamic and interactive stories.

Ryan acts as a fractional CTO and uses a swat team approach to each project, and expands his team only when it is necessary using a roster of remote developers and support staff. Ryan, as an individual, is available for remote projects and contracts as a fractional CTO & Principal Engineer. VersusMedia, as a team, is available for your full-service digital agency needs.
