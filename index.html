About

We offer innovative executive, software, and management solutions to startups and various industries including: artificial intelligence, entertainment, healthcare, manufacturing, food, and more. VersusMedia was founded by Ryan Vinson in 1999 after he worked for a number of dot-com companies across the US.

Ryan acts as a fractional CTO and uses a swat team approach to each project, and expands his team only when it is necessary using a roster of remote developers and support staff. Ryan, as an individual, is available for remote projects and contracts as a fractional CTO & Senior Engineer. VersusMedia, as a team, is available for your full-service digital agency needs.
